Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024-02-12

This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by Orion's Blog and how it is used. Our website address is: orionv.io.

What Information Do We Collect?

When contacting us or subscribing to our newsletter, you may be asked to enter your name, email address, or other details to help you with your experience.

How Do We Use Your Information?

The information we collect from you may be used in the following ways:

  • To personalize your experience and to allow us to deliver the type of content in which you are most interested.
  • To send periodic emails regarding our content or other products and services.

How Do We Protect Your Information?

We understand the importance of safeguarding your personal information and take the protection of such data seriously. While we utilize third-party services for certain operations, such as Buttondown for email newsletters and Formspree for form submissions, we select these partners based on their commitment to data security and privacy protection. Here is how we work together with these services to protect your information:

  • Partnering with Reputable Services: We choose services that are known for their robust security measures and compliance with industry standards to manage and protect your personal information.
  • Data Handling Practices: We ensure that our third-party service providers have policies and practices in place to protect the information they collect, process, or handle on our behalf.
  • Oversight and Compliance: We regularly review our third-party providers' privacy and security practices to ensure they comply with our standards and applicable laws.
  • Limited Access: Access to your personal information is restricted to authorized personnel and third-party service providers who need the information to perform their service obligations. These parties are bound by confidentiality agreements and are instructed to use the information solely in accordance with our privacy policy.
  • Information Security Awareness: We maintain awareness of information security best practices and require our third-party service providers to do the same.

Please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Do We Use 'Cookies'?

No, we do not use cookies for tracking purposes.

Third-party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information except as described below. We use third-party services, including Buttondown and Formspree, to manage email newsletter subscriptions and to process form submissions, respectively. These services are integral to our operations and are utilized to enhance our communication and service delivery. When you subscribe to our newsletter or submit information via forms on our website, your information is processed by these third-party services in accordance with their privacy policies and our directives. We ensure that these third parties are committed to protecting your information and limit their use of your personally identifiable information to the purposes for which it was provided to us.

Please note that these third parties have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review to understand their practices. Our relationship with these third parties does not constitute a sale, trade, or transfer of your personally identifiable information to outside parties beyond the necessary scope required for our services.

Contacting Us
